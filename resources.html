Resources

We believe every mother deserves support during the postpartum period—physically, emotionally, and financially. Below are resources to help you access care, find community, and reduce barriers to treatment.

Mental Health Support

Therapy

If you're experiencing anxiety, depression, stress, or emotional overwhelm after birth, speaking with a therapist can help.

We offer:

  • First 3 therapy sessions free
  • A free 30-minute introductory consultation
  • Referrals to low-cost therapy providers after the initial sessions

Therapy can help with:

  • Postpartum depression
  • Anxiety
  • Birth trauma
  • Relationship stress
  • Adjustment to parenthood

24/7 Support Hotline

If you need someone to talk to immediately, you can access a 24/7 support hotline for confidential support and crisis counseling.

Hotlines provide:

  • Immediate emotional support
  • Crisis counseling
  • Referrals to local resources

Alternative Therapy Options

Some parents find healing through complementary approaches alongside traditional medical care. We provide information and referrals for alternative therapies.

  • Yoga for stress reduction, gentle postpartum physical recovery, improving sleep and mood
  • Benefits: improves flexibility and strength, supports mental relaxation, helps regulate breathing and stress
  • Massage Therapy for muscle tension, postpartum physical discomfort, relaxation and stress relief
  • Benefits: improves circulation, reduces physical stress, promotes recovery and relaxation
  • Acupuncture for pain management, hormonal balance, stress and anxiety
  • Benefits: may reduce chronic pain, helps regulate body systems

Free Postpartum Health Screenings

Early detection saves lives. We offer free screenings for common postpartum health concerns:

  • Postpartum depression
  • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Cardiovascular complications

Screenings help identify warning signs early so you can receive proper care and treatment.

Financial Assistance

Apply for Assistance

We work to reduce financial barriers to postpartum care. Our assistance program may help cover:

  • Medications
  • Therapeutic services
  • Alternative medicine treatments
  • Telehealth visits
  • Follow-up postpartum care

Applications open on the first of every month.

Prescription Drug Assistance

If medication costs are high, patient assistance programs may reduce the cost of prescription drugs. These programs can provide:

  • Discounted medications
  • Free prescriptions for eligible patients
  • Long-term medication support

If our program is full: If assistance funding is currently unavailable, you can still access prescription savings.

Recommended options:

  • GoodRx coupon codes
  • Pharmacy discount programs

Expanded Care Services

We help connect mothers to services that are often overlooked during postpartum recovery. Available support includes:

  • Telehealth appointments for postpartum mental health
  • Postpartum physical therapy
  • Pelvic floor rehabilitation
  • Coverage for acupuncture and alternative medicine treatments

Practical Support

Childcare During Appointments

Access programs that provide childcare support so you can attend medical or therapy appointments without added stress.

Legal Aid

If you are facing medical bills or insurance disputes, legal aid services can help with:

  • Medical debt negotiation
  • Insurance claim disputes
  • Patient rights advocacy

If you have questions about any of these resources or would like help getting started, email us at hello@4thtrimester.org.
